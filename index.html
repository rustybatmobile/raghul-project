Biography

Mr Sulaiman Mathilakam from Kerala, India came to Dubai in search of opportunities in 2009. In just a few months, he started his first business venture called Master Cup café in 2009. Today in 2023, he has more than 22 outlets and over 150 staff members all around the globe working for his chain of Restaurants, Cafes, Supermarkets, Media and Real Estate Ventures. He’s not only in business, but also writes for different media and possesses skills for Short Filmmaking, Video Albums, Songs and many more.

He writes, acts and also produces short films.
"Ummande Shukur" is one of the short films produced by him. It has gained over 4 million views* and has become viral on Facebook and YouTube. He has also written over 50 poems.

Mr Sulaiman wrote a book called "Aiysha". The book is inspired by the concept of The Law of Attraction. His book was released at the November 2022 Sharjah international book fair. He also wrote a song for the Ruler of Dubai, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, and released it on the UAE National day. Besides his business, his talents in the media are also growing by day. All the hard work and commitment made possible by the support of his beloved family. His mother, Fathima Beevi, wife Rubeena, and his sons, Mohammad Fawaz and Mohammad Fayaz.
